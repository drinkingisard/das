Dellos Air Service - is onboard catering for business aviation since 2010.

DAS is a project of Maison Dellos, a benchmark for the quality of catering services in the field of transport. For 10 years of operation of the company, DAS has been a catering operator for more than 40,000 flights. The DAS menu includes dishes from all Maison Dellos restaurants: Cafe Pushkin, Turandot, Matryoshka, Fahrenheit, Bochka, Shinok and Kazbek. The team of professional chefs regularly updates the menu, focusing on seasonal and local products. Maison Dellos sets trends in modern gastronomy.

Our Philosophy


We managed to bring together industry professionals and create a unique catering project for private aviation. An experienced creative team of chefs creates first-class cuisine, "mixed with traditional French techniques and seasoned with fresh author's ideas."

When compiling the menu for business aviation, we proceeded from many years of experience within the industry, taking into account the wishes and comments of passengers and crew members.

We have combined high quality, variety of choices and affordable pricing. Ready meals are carefully packed in specialized and easy-to-transport boxes with stickers. The order also includes a parchment menu and step-by-step cooking instructions.

We are actively developing and expanding our geography of coverage. Our goal is to build a wide catering network in different cities and countries with a unified approach and high quality.
